Primary Level

Children from the age of 5-15 years old can join Madrasa Muhammadiya at the Primary Level, discovering the basics of their religion in a fun, interactive and engaging way.

At this important age and stage of their lives, we need them not only equipped with knowledge but to develop a love for deen and interest.

The right seeds and correct cultivation at this tender age can set the foundations of knowledge and character that will in sha Allah bear sweet fruits in years to come.

Learning Quran at this young age will be supplemented with Islamic stories and games, while encouraging gender learning and exercising themselves. With the basics of Arabic reading, Quranic reading and memorization of surahs and duas taught in an engaging and fun way.



Top Quality Islamic Education

The Quran is the Word of our Creator and part of His identity and His truth. Allah هلالج لج is the Creator of the universe and every creation within it. The Quran is written in Arabic therefore; however, we believe our children must understand what they read to stay connected with their Creator.

Therefore the Quran, Sunnah, Islamic Studies (Aqaid) are all taught at Madrasa Muhammadiya in an engaging, fun and unique manner with hands-on activities that our children can relate to, bring alive in the 3D universe, maximize their daily spiritual experience, increase emotions of love and respect to our deen.

With small class sizes, every student at Madrasa Muhammadiya will receive personal care and attention from teachers. The courses and classes run flexible at their own pace, achieving positive outcomes – We guarantee that Insha Allah our classes are therefore, fun, positive and beneficial to all students.

Islamic Education in the comfort of your own home. Anywhere in the world.

No more dropping and picking the kids to and from madrasa! Save time and money!

The curriculum at Madrasa Muhammadiya includes Quran, Duas, Names of the Prophets, Pillars of Islam and Iman, Morals and Manners and much more, taught in a fun, interactive and relevant way.

Give your child a Head Start for Life!

At Madrasa Muhammadiya, we aim to instill in each student a true understanding and love for their deen that will stand them in good stead for the rest of their lives.

Seeking knowledge starts from the cradle and ends at the grave. We want all our students to have the best means to strive and succeed in their learning throughout their lives, displaying the beautiful taught by our beloved Prophet ﷺ
