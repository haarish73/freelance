Why Madrasa Muhammadiya?

We aim to revolutionise Islamic learning for children and youth of all ages.

Gone are the days of being forced to sit through hours of uninspiring and laborious sessions in an attempt to be able to read the Words of Allah ﷻ and learn the basics of His Religion.

We believe that Islamic learning should be inspirational and fun for our children and youth.

Developing a lasting love for Allah ﷻ, His Messenger ﷺ, His Book, and His Beautiful Religion that will stand them in good stead to face the challenges of life in a difficult environment.

What is an Online Madrasa?

A madrasa — but not as we know it!

A madrasa is traditionally used to refer to after school or weekend classes to teach young children the Quran and occasionally some Islamic studies. A wonderful initiative that has educated millions of children in the recitation and memorisation of the Words of Allah. Our online madrasa aims to revolutionise this form of learning by providing an efficient, fun and engaging platform for children to excel in.

Virtual Learning Environment
  • Students and parents simply log into the Madrasa Muhammadiya Website Portal
  • Live virtual classroom with whiteboard, video, and audio
  • Interactive chatbox for communication
Interactive Features

Using voice, text, whiteboard, notes, recordings, presentations and screen sharing, lessons are much more engaging and interactive than at traditional madrasas. Students can be set tasks to work through specific exercises, share their work or demonstrate to the class.

This allows every student the chance to directly and privately communicate with the teacher, so no one is ever left feeling missed out or left behind.
